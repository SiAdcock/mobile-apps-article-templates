Football Weekly Extra: Jürgen Klopp gets his first Liverpool win ahead of Chelsea test

About this content
The podders look back on a midweek of shocks and penalty shoot-outs in the League Cup. Plus, previews of Liverpool's trip to Chelsea and the rest of the big games in the Premier League

On today's Football Weekly Extra, AC Jimbo is joined by Barry Glendenning, James Horncastle and the lesser-spotted Paul Doyle to look back on a midweek of shocks, shoot-outs and much-needed wins in the Capital One Cup.

Jürgen Klopp got his first win as Liverpool manager as the Reds bested Bournemouth, while Sheffield Wednesday did a number of Arsenal and Middlesbrough held their nerve at Old Trafford to knock out Manchester United. Ouch.

Chelsea also lost on penalties, the latest defeat coming at Stoke, and the next possibly this weekend at home to Liverpool. How long does Mou have left to moan before he is finally given the heave-ho?

Finally, after rounding up a cracking round of games in Serie A - no, really - we look ahead to the rest of games in the Premier League, including a chance for West Ham to go top. West Ham!
