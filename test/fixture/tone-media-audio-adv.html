In the first episode, bandmates Laura and Greg talk with singer Danielle Ponder about juggling schedules, keeping tight budgets and staying true to their visions

Ever wonder what it's really like to balance a day job with being in a band? Or are you caught in a fine-tuned balancing act yourself? Either way, here's a dose of real talk from Laura O'Neill and Greg Yagolnitzer of the indie pop band Laura and Greg, and Danielle Ponder of the soul group Danielle Ponder and the Tomorrow People.

By day, New York City-based O'Neill is the co-founder of Van Leeuwen Artisan Ice Cream and the co-owner of Selamat Pagi, an Indonesian restaurant in Brooklyn, while Yagolnitzer is a visual artist who recently co-directed a Weezer music video. Ponder, who's based in Rochester, New York, is a time-strapped public defender for Monroe County. In this podcast episode, the performers get together to discuss how they keep their day jobs and side hustles in perfect harmony.
