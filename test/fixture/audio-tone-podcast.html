Our film team brings you the latest news and reviews from the Cannes 2016 film festival

The latest from the team at the Cannes film festival. Day six is headlined by Jeff Nichols’ Loving, the true story of Richard and Mildred Loving, a white man (played by Joel Edgerton) and a black woman (Ruth Negga) who’s marriage was declared illegal by the state of Virginia in 1958. Next up, Paterson, Jim Jarmusch’s kooky drama about a bus driver (Adam Driver) who detours into a sideline as a minimalist poet. And finally, Hell or High Water, a crime thriller starring Chris Pine and Ben Foster as bank robbing brothers on the run from a veteran Texas ranger (Jeff Bridges).
