Standfirst

Used to display the standfirst of an article. Any emboldened text is normalised within this style.

While riders will have some freedom to move, they won't have the ability to move without others watching and storing their data

<div class="article__standfirst">
    <p>While riders will have some freedom to move, they won't have the ability to move without others watching and storing their data</p>
</div>
